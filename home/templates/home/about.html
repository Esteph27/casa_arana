Our Casa

We're here to introduce Peruvian homewear and decor into your homes. We're a place where you can find exquisite and exclusive homewear pieces that are sourced directly from talented artisans from across Peru.

We're not your usual homewear store where products are made in factories, instead we source our products from a handful of carefully selected artisans (or makers) meaning all of our products are sold for a limited time only, and most importantly, are not mass produced. We believe true craftsmanship should be celebrated, not replicated. We believe we should support our Artisans (who often still work with ancient Incan techniques) to bring them the business their skills and creativity demand. Our Artisans have provided some of the most beautiful and unique pieces we have seen and we want to introduce them to the world, in a fair and honest way.

What we sell is part of a bigger picture, we don't just sell beautiful pieces for the home, we also provide support to the artisan community of Peru to help maintain their craft and their individual businesses. Each item sold here holds a story, a history and a purpose. We're so proud to be able to do what we do and we hope that you're able to find something special for your home.

How we Work

Here we celebrate the labour of love and unique design, it's incredibly important to us that we support our artisans as much as possible. Most of our artisans have been refining their craft for generations and it's sad to see their craft end because they're not getting the income needed to sustain their business, or family. As well as this, we have seen cheap imitations in the western world and although are happy to see Peruvian design become more accessible, we do not believe in mass production. We're here to bring the power back to the marker, give them the business opportunity they deserve to continue sharing their craft and help them support their families.

We take great pride in the relationships we have you with our artisans, they're not just our makers, but most importantly, our clients. We ensure complete transparency along our supply chain buying directly buying from our artisans, no middle men involved. When it's time to buy new products, we head out to visit out artisans and collectively curate a collection to sell together. It's a collaboration where the artisans are as involved as we are and its our mission to keep it that way.

Support More

However, if you'd like to support our artisans and other communities in Peru then we would be most humbled if you could extend your support by donating to one of our selected charities below.
